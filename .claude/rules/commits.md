## Commits

Use conventional commits format: `type(scope): description`

Types: `feat`, `fix`, `refactor`, `docs`, `test`, `chore`, `style`, `perf`, `ci`, `build`, `revert`

Focus on WHY not WHAT. First line under 72 chars. Body for detail if needed.
